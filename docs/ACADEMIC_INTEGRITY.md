# Academic Integrity Guardrails

EssayCraft should be framed as a writing-process assistant, not an essay mill.

## Must do

- Help students see essay structure.
- Explain why a segment is background/thesis/evidence/analysis/etc.
- Mark missing citations.
- Encourage paraphrasing in the student's own words.
- Preserve drafts, patches, and snapshots as a visible process.

## Must not do

- Invent sources.
- Hide AI use.
- Produce untraceable final essays with fake references.
- Replace the student's agency.

## Citation policy for MVP

If the app cannot verify a source, it must display `[citation needed]` or `issue`. Real citation generation belongs in a later source-search/reference-manager phase.

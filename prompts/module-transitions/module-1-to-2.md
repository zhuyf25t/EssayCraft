# Module 1 -> Module 2: Topic & Question to Research & Evidence

Purpose: convert topic, research question, early thesis, and stance into a research/evidence plan.

Output: refined question, working thesis, 3-4 argument branches, evidence needs, source types, keywords, CARS reminder, and missing-evidence warnings.

Formatting: headings and bullets with blank lines between sections.

Citation behavior: never invent sources; use `[citation needed]` or source-type placeholders.

Failure behavior: if input is thin, return a short research plan and visible evidence gaps.

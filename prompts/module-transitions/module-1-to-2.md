# Module 1 -> Module 2: Topic & Question to Research & Evidence

Purpose: convert topic, research question, early thesis, and stance into a research/evidence plan.

Output: research plan for the student's actual topic, research question, working thesis, 3-4 argument branches, evidence to look for, source types, keywords, source status, CARS reminder, and source-needed planning notes.

Formatting: headings and bullets with blank lines between sections.

Citation behavior: never invent sources. Module 2 is a planning module, so prefer `source needed` language over `[citation needed]`.

Failure behavior: if input is thin, return a short research plan and visible source needs without fabricating evidence.
